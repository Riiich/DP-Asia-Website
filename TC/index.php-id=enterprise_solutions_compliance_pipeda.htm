DigitalPersona Compliance Solutions - PIPEDA

Canadian Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)

The Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) is similar in intent to European Commission's EU Directives on data privacy. PIPEDA specifies the rules that organizations must adhere to in collecting, using or disclosing personal information. It also recognizes the rights of individuals with respect to use of their personal information.

PIPEDA applies to organizations and their employees in the transportation, communications, banking, government and other sectors. PIPEDA requires organizations to:

  • Obtain consent before collecting, using, and disclosing any personal information.
  • Collect personal information in reasonable, appropriate, and lawful ways.
  • Establish personal information policies that are clear, reasonable, and ready to protect individuals' personal information.

Identity Compliance and Privacy Laws

Identity security is fundamental to meeting privacy law requirements. DigitalPersona's products are critical to maintaining compliance with PIPEDA's requirements:

Strong Passwords

  • Provide stronger safeguards against unauthorized access to personal information regardless of storage location or format; ensure that only individuals with "need-to-know" can access personal information.

Secure Communications

  • Protect information and communications through encryption, prevent unauthorized disclosure, access or copying.

Multi-Factor Authentication

  • Safeguard confidentiality of personal data; use strong authentication to ensure only designated employees access particularly sensitive data.

Transaction Accountability

  • Protect personal data against accidental or unlawful access, copying or modification by tagging each data transaction with initiator's identity.
  • Enable fine-grained accountability of every access to personal data records.
